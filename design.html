Typography

Broadsides posters

These are informational poster designs that are meant to highlight a chosen typeface. The posters follow a specific format, and include the following elements:

The name of the creator; the date it was released; the name of the typeface; a display using all the common characters; and a short blurb about the history of this typeface.

I had to go through several versions of each of these designs. I focused mainly on experimenting with different compositions. The "Helvetica" poster specifically was given rigid specifications on the size, weight, and angle of the text.
