A Little About Me!

Hi, I'm Sree! I'm a student at Northeastern University, currently studying Computer Science and Interaction Design.

My passions lie within the interaction between humans and computers, and I find the most importance in human-centered design. In terms of personal projects I'm currently focusing on web and software development, and hoping to learn more about algorithms too. I'm also always working on illustration projects. When it comes to art, I like to concentrate on character design and conveying narrative. At the moment I'm juggling between a couple of comics and stories that I'm hoping to publish. A big dream of mine is to create a printed graphic novel series ^_^ Ultimately I'd like to find a middle point between my interest in art and tech.
